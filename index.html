Pete and the Beanstalk

Join Pete the Penguin on his magical adventure climbing the beanstalk and collecting beautiful flowers!

Game Features

  • Guide Pete the Penguin up the magical beanstalk
  • Test your reflexes with quick teleportation mechanics
  • Collect beautiful flowers along your journey
  • Simple yet challenging gameplay
  • Compete for the highest score

How to Play

  • Press A or Left Arrow to teleport left
  • Press D or Right Arrow to teleport right
  • Collect as many flowers as possible
  • Avoid falling off the beanstalk
  • Press ESC to quit the game

About the Game

Inspired by the classic tale of Jack and the Beanstalk, Pete the Penguin plants his own magical seed and embarks on an exciting vertical adventure. Your goal is to help our loveable penguin reach the pretty pink flower at the top while collecting as many flowers as possible along the way. With simple controls but challenging gameplay, this charming game will test your reflexes and timing!
